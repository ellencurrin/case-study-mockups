Case Studies

Citywide Property Surveys

Tracking Blight in Detroit, MI

In a project called Motor City Mapping, Site Control and the LOVELAND App enabled 150+ Detroiters to survey 400,000 properties in just 35 days of surveying. An unprecedented project, Motor City Mapping informed land use planning while Detroit went through the largest municipal bankruptcy in American history. It helped the city, investors, and neighborhood groups address everything from real estate development to hundreds of millions of dollars worth of blight removal and demolition. Check out the project at motorcitymapping.org

The Best Surveying Tool in Ohio

After surveying six cities with a different mapping platform, Thriving Communities Institute (TCI) in Cleveland, Ohio switched to Site Control because of the reliability and stability of our technology, as well as the user-friendly interface. TCI then used Site Control to survey every property in Cleveland and Dayton, Ohio. Read more about the survey in Cleveland here.

Neighborhood Groups

Create Work Plans and Measure Impact

Focus:HOPE used LOVELAND tools to create maps, work plans, and strategize for a three-day event where thousands of volunteers worked on a beautifying initiative. They also utilized the LOVELAND app to to document progress during the event. Learn More.

Create a Neighborhood Property Inventory

Grandmont Rosedale Development Corporation annually surveys properties in their service area and use this data to identify, track, and map vacant properties. They also use Site Control to record the property intervention performed by the Vacant Property Task Force operating in their neighborhood.

Use Data to Allocate Resources

Pittsburgh Community Reinvestment Group used LOVELAND software to survey the Bloomfield neighborhood in Pittsburgh. The data collected will help local nonprofits and residents connect with resources that will improve and repair homes and lead to a more sustainable neighborhood for everyone. Learn More.

Government Departments

Water and Sewage Service

Detroit Water and Sewage Department uses Site Control to keep track of which houses have received notices for late payments and other warnings.

Property Tax Foreclosure

The Wayne County Treasurer used LOVELAND tools to survey foreclosed homes and collect useful data that can inform future policies and programs. The Treasurer also uses Site Control to help plan and implement initiatives designed to stabilize neighborhoods.
